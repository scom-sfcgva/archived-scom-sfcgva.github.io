About us

Are you descerning for a service role in the community that allows you to express creative freedom and help with the mission of evangelizing through social media, photos, videos, and other means of communication? SFC Communications (SCOM) is exactly what you're looking for!

SCOM, SFC Communications, is the creative and communication arm for Singles for Christ Canada. At a national level, a group of talents young Catholics come together to use their time, talent, and treasure to help the community flourish.
